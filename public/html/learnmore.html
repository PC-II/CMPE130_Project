Learn More About Our AI Analysis

Our AI-powered tool provides quick, affordable insights into potential structural issues. Designed for homeowners, landlords, and construction companies, this tool makes it easy to assess integrity concerns by analyzing uploaded images.

How It Works

1. Answer a Few Questions: To help us understand your needs, you will be asked to answer a few questions about the structure and the issues you are experiencing.

2. Upload an Image: Upload a clear image of the structure you want to assess.

3. AI Analysis: Our AI detects issues such as cracks or water damage in the uploaded image.

4. Receive Recommendations: You will receive repair recommendations tailored to the issue based on the analysis.
